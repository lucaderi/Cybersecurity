# ntop PCAP Repositories

- **URL:** https://github.com/ntop/nDPI/tree/dev/tests/cfgs/default/pcap
- **Description:** Real-world traffic PCAPs from protocols detected by nDPI

# Training & Research PCAP Repositories

## 1. Zeek/Malware Traffic Analysis
- **URL:** https://www.malware-traffic-analysis.net/
- **Description:** Excellent source of real-world malware traffic exercises with PCAPs
- **Recommended for:** All three exercises - has baseline, infection, and exfiltration scenarios

## 2. Stratosphere Linux IPS PCAP Datasets
- **URL:** https://www.stratosphereips.org/datasets-overview/
- **Description:** Academic datasets with labeled malicious and normal traffic
- **Recommended for:** Exercise 1 (baseline vs anomaly detection)

## 3. CICIDS2017 Dataset
- **URL:** https://www.unb.ca/cic/datasets/ids-2017.html
- **Description:** Comprehensive dataset with normal and attack traffic
- **Recommended for:** All exercises, especially baseline establishment

## 4. Wireshark Sample Captures
- **URL:** https://wiki.wireshark.org/SampleCaptures
- **Description:** Large collection of various traffic types
- **Recommended for:** Building baseline understanding

## 5. CTU University Malware Capture Dataset
- **URL:** https://mcfp.felk.cvut.cz/publicDatasets/
- **Description:** Real botnet traffic captures
- **Recommended for:** Exercise 2 (data exfiltration analysis)
